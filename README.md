# IndusKnow – AI Industrial Knowledge Intelligence Platform

IndusKnow is an AI-powered Industrial Knowledge Intelligence Platform designed to eliminate knowledge fragmentation across industrial operations. The platform unifies engineering documents, maintenance records, inspection reports, safety procedures, and compliance data into a single intelligent system that enables faster decision-making, improved operational efficiency, and enhanced regulatory compliance.

## Problem Statement

Industrial organizations often store critical information across disconnected systems, making it difficult for engineers, maintenance teams, and operators to access the right information at the right time. This results in increased downtime, compliance risks, and loss of valuable institutional knowledge.

IndusKnow addresses this challenge by transforming scattered industrial data into a searchable, actionable, and continuously updated knowledge ecosystem.
